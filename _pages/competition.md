## National Level

**[China University Students' Mechanical Engineering Innovation and Creativity Competition — Intelligent Manufacturing (Industrial AI)](/Junhao_Feng.github.io/awards/mechanical-innovation-2024/)**
*Aug. 2024 — National First Prize*
- Focused on Industrial Internet and Industrial AI.
- My role: in a real-world gear manufacturing scenario with large-scale parameters, used classic machine learning methods (e.g., Random Forest, k-NN) for defect judgement; also implemented industrial vision algorithms (QR code recognition, edge detection, etc.) with OpenCV under varying environments.
- Team: Junhao Feng, AoZhi Wang, Xinyi Chen
- Advisors: Assoc. Prof. Chunyue Lu; Assoc. Prof. Huiying Ban
